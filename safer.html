Frequently Asked Question's (FAQ'S)

What type of services does Safer provide?

Safer provides controlled, monitored, safe and affordable commute for your office, school/college/university/ coaching centres and events( Weddings/picnics). Safer upgrades the Vehicles of transport service providers to ensure Control and Monitoring, and Safer App Platform bridges the Gap between Transport Service Providers and Routine commuters.

Which areas does Safer presently cover?

We have started our Services in Karachi presently with routes for various destinations. Please download our application from Google Play Store or Apple App Store. Become a Safer member today to know and choose the route that suits you best.

What are the modes of payment at Safer?

You can either pay easily through jazz cash or easy paisa using below details. Or on the account numbers given below. Alternately, cash payment option is also available.

How do I make a booking?

Bookings with Safer are on monthly basis. Safer App allows seamless complete booking transaction starting from New user registration to vehicle options, completing booking process with payment options and Invoice being received successfully through email. You can also see the tutorial for registration by opening the link below.

What precautionary measures are Safer taking for COVID-19?

In the light of COVID-19, Safer assures its customers that all mandatory SOPs are being thoroughly followed to safeguard against any possible exposure. This includes: -Thermal testing: Only users with normal body temperature will be allowed to board the vehicle. Our drivers are provided with contact less digital infrared thermometer temperature guns. -Mandatory sanitizing: Every van carries a sanitizing console. For each rider and driver, sanitizing hands will be mandatory before proceeding to their seats. -Regular disinfection: To ensure a virus-free cabin, the interiors of the vans will be regularly disinfected. -Additionally, Face Masks are readily available in all our service providing vehicles.

How early do I need to book?

You need to book 15 days prior to the start of your route. Once booking is done and Operations started, its Recurring for all forthcoming months, till the booking is cancelled.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking.

How can I cancel my booking?

You simply have to go to your booking page in your app and select cancellation option to cancel your booking for the next month.

Can we travel to other cities?

Intercity travel can only be possible on weekends with our events services. However this service is not yet launched.

How do I know if my reservation is confirmed?

Once you complete your registration through our application, you will get a confirmation SMS on your mobile phone. Your selected driver shall visit your premises 2 or 3 days prior to the actual start of the services and to have orientation.

There are three choices of vehicles A/c and non A/c with safer -7 seaters -16 seaters -30 seaters. Using our application you will see the available vehicles that suit your route in the best possible manner.

How are prices calculated?

There are various factors that are involved in pricing. while booking through our app you will see a calculator through which you will get an estimate for your ride. It is KM and time based.

Who will monitor the CCTV footage?

Safer take special care about Preserving individuals On-Board Privacy and holds Zero Tolerance Policy towards Privacy Breach. This is the reason we do not give access of CCTV monitoring to Parents/ Guardians and holds such monitoring access to very limited female Customer Support Representatives. Recording of all the CCTV cameras are solely for Surveillance and security purpose and would be shared only with concerned Parents/ Guardians and/or law enforcement agencies, had there been any such event/ incident/ situation arising. The CCTV recording, otherwise will be automatically deleted after 15 days. Relevant footage and screenshots may be shared upon request, in case needed. (please refer our Privacy Policy for further details).
